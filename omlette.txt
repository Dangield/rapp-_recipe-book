three eggs
black pepper
one tablespoon of water
two tablespoons of butter
two teaspoons of mustard
one tablespoon of sour cream
one ounce of chopped ham
@
Beat eggs with salt, pepper and water.
Heat butter in a pan over high heat until frothing.
Pour in egg mix.
Turn down heat slightly.
Gently push cooked egg to the middle of pan with a wooden fork, allowing uncooked egg to run underneath.
When omelette is set on top - and browned underneath, spread with mustard, sour cream and sprinkle with chopped ham.
Flip omelette over and slide onto a serving plate.
